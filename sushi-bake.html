Sushi Bake

Description

Ingredients

  • Imitation Crab
  • Avocado
  • Cream Cheese
  • Sushi Rice
  • Rice Vinegar
  • Furikake
  • Kewpie Mayo

Steps

  1. Make Sushi Rice
  2. Put Sushi Rice in Baking Tray
  3. Mix Mayo, Cream Cheese, and Imitation Crab
  4. Put Mixture on top of Rice
  5. Bake 10 Minutes Medium Heat
  6. Top with Avocado
